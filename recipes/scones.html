Scones

Description

Makes approximately six delicious vegan scones in just 20 minutes!

Credit to Parsley Soup Vegan Recipes.

Ingredients

  • 225g self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 15g sugar
  • 40g vegan margarine
  • 150ml soya (or other non-dairy) milk
  • Optional strawberry/raspberry jam and vegan whipping cream, to serve

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 200C/gas 6
  2. Mix the flour and baking powder, and rub in the margarine until no lumps. Then stir in the sugar.
  3. Add enough of the soya milk to mix to a slightly sticky dough, leaving a little bit of the milk for glazing.
  4. Roll out the dough on a floured board (to about 1.5cm thick), then cut out the scones with a 2 1/2" cutter. Place on a lightly greased baking sheet and brush the tops with the left-over milk.
  5. Cook for about 15 minutes, until risen and lightly browned.
  6. Serve with jam and cream, if desired. And enjoy!
