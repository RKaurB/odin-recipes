Yorkshire puddings

Description

Finally, vegan Yorkshire Puddings that turn out perfectly every time!

Credit to BBC Good Food.

Ingredients

  • 225g self-raising flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 300 ml unsweetened soya milk (or other non-dairy milk)
  • 100ml warm water
  • 8 tsp vegetable oil
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Mix together the flour, baking powder, milk, water, and salt. Then transfer the mixture to a jug, cover with cling-film, and leave in fridge for around 30 minutes.
  2. Pre-heat oven to 220C/gas 7.
  3. Spoon a tsp of oil into eight holes of a muffin tin, and place in oven for 5 minutes to get really hot.
  4. Remove tin from oven carefully, pour the batter into the hot oil, and place back in theoven.
  5. Bake for 23-30 minutes until risen and golden brown.
  6. Enjoy!
